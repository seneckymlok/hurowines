A Heritage of Excellence

Hungarian wine used to be among the most prized in Europe. 100 years ago it would have been offered at every Royal Court. Wine has been produced in Hungary from Roman times, with influences from both the East and the West creating a unique winemaking tradition.

Today, the Hungarian wine industry is quietly re-establishing itself, returning to traditional methods whilst embracing technological advances. HURO Winery concentrates on small, premium quality producers, bringing their exceptional wines to those who wish to explore and expand their horizons.

From Royal Courts to Your Table

For centuries, Hungarian wines graced the tables of European nobility. Despite the challenges of the Phylloxera epidemic and decades of industrial production, the spirit of Hungarian winemaking has endured.

Today, passionate winemakers in Hungary and Transylvania are reviving traditional methods and producing wines of exceptional quality. We are proud to support these artisans and bring their finest creations to discerning wine enthusiasts.
