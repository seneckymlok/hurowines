The Delicate Flavour of Pure Acacia

Bringing you the delicate flavour of pure acacia honey directly from the sprawling acacia forests of Hungary and Transylvania. Rich with antioxidants, antibiotics and immune-boosting enzymes, authentic Hungarian acacia honey is packed with natural health benefits not found in other honey blends.

Who We Are

Bee HURO Honey is a health-focused honey wholesaler with roots in Hungary, Transylvania, and West Romania. We look to bring authentic, pure, and natural honey to a new UK market.

We are a team of honey-loving experts who work directly with local, family-owned beekeeping businesses to bring pure Hungarian acacia honey to our UK audience. Our deep ties with the region and personal connection with our suppliers mean that we can bring you the highest quality product at more affordable prices.

Traditional Production Excellence

Bee HURO Honey is produced from bees kept by specialist beekeepers in Hungary and Hungarian producers in west Romania in traditional ways handed down from father to son and is extremely pure.
